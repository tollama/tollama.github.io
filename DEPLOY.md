# Deploy (GitHub Pages)

1. Copy the `docs/` folder into the root of your `tollama/tollama` repo.
2. On GitHub: **Settings → Pages**
3. **Source:** Deploy from a branch
4. **Branch:** `main` (or default) · **Folder:** `/docs`
5. Save.

Your site will be available at: `https://tollama.github.io/tollama/`
